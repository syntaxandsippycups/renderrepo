Welcome to Syntax & Sippy Cups

Welcome to Syntax and Sippy Cups — where creative code meets sticky fingers and soul-deep reflections. This is a cozy corner of the internet for the tender and tired, the curious and creative — especially the mamas building both babies and dreams. Here's what you’ll find here:

  • Honest reflections on faith and motherhood — raw, vulnerable blog posts from the sacred (and sometimes sloppy) middle.
  • Digital art — playful, personal visuals born from naptime bursts of creativity and a love for color and code.
  • Poetry — quiet, heartfelt pieces written in the in-between moments that speak to grace, identity, and presence.

This is a space for showing up with steady love — for creating, nurturing, and trusting that even the smallest offerings matter. “May the favor of the Lord our God rest on us; establish the work of our hands for us — yes, establish the work of our hands.” - Psalm 90:17

Welcome to the mess, the beauty, and the story in progress.
